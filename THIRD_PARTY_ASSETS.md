# Third-Party Assets

Color Dash Galaxy was built with a mix of original work and third-party assets.
This file documents what was used and explains why some assets are **not** included
in this public repository.

The licenses of the paid Unity Asset Store packs below do **not** permit
redistribution of their files — including modified or derived versions. To respect
those terms, those assets and any art derived from them have been removed from this
repository. The compiled game (where assets are baked into the build, which the
Asset Store EULA permits) is available to play on itch.io:

> [eleceb.itch.io/color-dash-galaxy](https://eleceb.itch.io/color-dash-galaxy)

## Removed from this repository

The following assets are **not** included here. To use them, obtain them from their
original source under their own license.

| Asset | Author | Used for | Source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pixel Art Spaceships for SHMUP | Dylestorm | Player ship, enemy ships, bullets, thrusters, projectiles | https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/characters/pixel-art-spaceships-for-shmup-131191 |
| Pixel Enemies for SHMUP | Dylestorm | Boss sprites | https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/2d/characters/pixel-enemies-for-shmup-219887 |
| Ultimate Particle Pack | AS Gaming Studio | Explosion and particle effects | https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/vfx/particles/ultimate-particle-pack-265409 |

Derived works made from the packs above (e.g. recoloured/cropped boss parts,
recoloured enemy and boss projectiles, and the boss explosion effect built on the
particle pack's textures) have also been removed, since derivatives of these assets
cannot be redistributed either.

As a result, opening this project in the Unity editor will show missing references
on some prefabs and scenes. This is expected — the complete experience is the
itch.io build.
